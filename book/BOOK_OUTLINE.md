# AI Ops: The Operating Manual for the Intelligence Age

> A Book by FrankX

## Concept

This isn't a book about AI tools. It's about how you **operate** in a world where AI is your constant collaborator. From morning routines to creative projects, from family life to enterprise work—AI Ops is the practice that ties it all together.

## Target Audience

- **Creators** wanting to 10x output without losing authenticity
- **Entrepreneurs** building AI-native businesses
- **Families** navigating AI's role in household life
- **Knowledge workers** drowning in tools, seeking integration
- **Leaders** making AI decisions for teams/orgs
